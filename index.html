PENN PLAY 2016

You have one weekend. Come play.

Penn Play is a 24-hour student-run game jam celebrating graphics, programming, art, music, writing, and a culture of collaboration and play in gaming. Every spring, over 100 students from all schools participate in Penn Play. They build a game from scratch over the course of the weekend and enjoy tutorials, free food and sponsor swag, and a lot of fun side events.

Last year, Penn Play welcomed Unity, Zynga, and Riot Games as sponsors, and hosted talks from the director of Drexel University’s game design program and scholars researching feminism and the representation of women in gaming communities.

Originally founded by DMD students in 2013, Penn Play has grown into a 20-strong team of students from across Penn’s schools.
